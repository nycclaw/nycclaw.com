Frequently Asked Questions

What is OpenClaw?
OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant platform that connects to every major AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, and more). Unlike ChatGPT or Claude alone, OpenClaw lets you switch between models, keeps your data private, and integrates with your email, calendar, and workflows.
Why do I need a setup service?
OpenClaw is powerful but requires technical configuration — API keys, integrations, model selection, privacy settings, and workflow automation. Our service handles all of that so you can start using AI immediately, without touching a terminal or reading documentation.
Is my data private?
Yes. OpenClaw runs on your own devices. Your conversations, files, and data stay under your control. We configure it with privacy-first settings and can set up fully local AI models if you want zero cloud dependency.
Do I need my own AI subscriptions?
We help you set up API keys with the providers you choose. Most people spend $20–50/month on AI API usage. We'll recommend the right providers based on your needs and help you create accounts during setup.
What happens after setup?
You get follow-up support (2 weeks for Remote, 1 month for In-Person) to answer questions and fine-tune your setup. After that, you're fully independent — OpenClaw is yours. We also host a NYC community with meetups and a Discord for ongoing help.
Is the $50 discovery call worth it?
Absolutely. You'll walk away with a clear picture of which AI tools fit your workflow, estimated monthly costs, and a setup plan — even if you don't proceed with our service. The $50 also credits toward any setup package.
